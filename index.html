About Us

We are an Indonesian tropical fruit exporter committed to delivering freshness and premium quality to the global market. Our company was founded from the awareness of the abundant potential of tropical fruits, starting with pineapples widely cultivated in Southeast Sulawesi. From this opportunity, we expanded our vision to supply a wide variety of tropical fruits to international buyers worldwide.
